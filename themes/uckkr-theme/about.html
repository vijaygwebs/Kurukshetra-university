Welcome to University College

University college Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra is a co-educational institution offering courses upto undegraduate level. It enjoys the singular distinction of being a college maintained by Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra, which is well-reputed at the national level in general and in North India in particular. The college deservedly prides itself on having a well-qualified teaching staff, a vastly spacious playground and profusely equipped science laboratories. The wholly computerized library with a vast collection of books and journals with internet facility to the users maintains its unstinted records of being a holy place for the acquisition of knowledge for those who pursue perfection and excellence. Besides, the college has lush green lawns with shadowy trees and fragrant flowers adding to its exotic locale. It is housed in one of the oldest and artistically planned buildings.

It has come up on the most hygienic and pollution-free environment of the University Campus. It is because of these rare merits that the college attracts a huge number of admission seekers every year from all over the country. Those seeking admissions have to compete on merit and the admitted ones rightly feel exalted by entering this great and incomparable institution of undergraduate studies. The reason being, they enjoy the added advantage of being a part and parcel of the student fraternity on the Campus.They have free access to books in the University Library and have a direct communion with the University teachers. There are two hostels - one for boys and another for girls - specifically ear-marked for the students admitted to the college. The hostel facilities are just remarkable as the students get an opportunity to reside in the healthiest environment pulsating with glorious academic, cultural and sports traditions. The college has all the three faculties viz. Arts, Commerce and Science for the undergraduate students.

Vision

Kurukshetra Be globally acknowledged as a distinguished centre of academic excellence.

The Kurukshetra University was established in 1956 as a unitary residential University and its foundation stone was laid by late Dr. Rajendra Prasad, the first President of India. Located in the holy city of Kurukshetra, land of the historical battle of 'Mahabharata' and the great message of Bhagwad Gita, its campus is situated on the western bank of Brahm Sarover (the holy tank) and extends over an area of over 400 acres.
